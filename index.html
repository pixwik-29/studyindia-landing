Our Transparent Admission Roadmap

A structured, stress-free pathway mapping how we collaborate with students and parents from initial signup to college enrollment.

1

Initial Consultation

Schedule a personal counseling intake at our Chennai head office or online. We evaluate your NEET expectations, state eligibility, and budget constraints.

2

College Identification

Receive a personalized report listing top-fit medical colleges (SRM, SRMC, Saveetha, Vels, etc.) aligning with your performance and preferences.

3

Counseling Management

Our research desk administers your registrations, choices, and option locks under MCC and State Selection Committees (DME Tamil Nadu) to maximize seat outcomes.

4

Seat Allotment & Selection

Secure the seat allotment letter. We run immediate audits on fee structures, NRI credentials if applicable, and physical joining guidelines.

5

Enrollment Confirmation

Accompany you for the college reporting steps. Confirm the final joining report and welcome your future as an aspiring medical specialist!
